Tell Your Story

Life After is an innovative, survivor-driven TV series that aims to offer hope and resources for individuals facing mental health challenges. Starting with depression, the show will feature raw, personal stories of recovery, allowing viewers to relate and learn about various mental health treatment paths in an unbiased, non-commercialized manner. The program will expand to become a broader platform for mental health education and support. Proceeds will be donated to support mental health initiatives.

  • Job Loss
  • Grief
  • Addiction
  • Bullying
  • Suicidal Ideation
  • And more...

If you or someone you know would like to help by sharing your story, we would love to hear from you.

We understand how sensitive this is.
Every submission is private and reviewed with care. You'll only move forward if you're comfortable.

  • Anonymous to start
  • Trauma-informed interviewing
  • Safe, judgment-free process
  • Guided by a respectful film team
  • You're in control at every step
  • Professional mental health support available
