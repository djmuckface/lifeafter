Your Story Matters

We're looking for brave individuals who have walked through the darkness of mental health challenges and found their way to healing and hope. Your journey—whether it involved depression, anxiety, trauma, addiction, grief, or any other struggle—has the power to inspire and guide others who are still fighting their battles.

About Life After

An innovative, survivor-driven TV series offering hope and resources for individuals facing mental health challenges. Starting with depression, our show features raw, personal stories of recovery, allowing viewers to relate and learn about various mental health treatment paths in an unbiased, non-commercialized manner. The program will expand to become a broader platform for mental health education and support, with proceeds donated to support mental health initiatives.
