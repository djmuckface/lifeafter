Your Story Matters

Seeking courageous individuals who have navigated the darkness of mental health challenges and emerged stronger on the other side. Have you, or someone you know, found a path to a healthier, happier, more fulfilling life? Your journey out of anxiety, trauma, PTSD, addiction, grief, abuse, rape, OCD, extreme life challanges - or any other struggle has the power to inspire and guide others who are still fighting their battle.

By sharing your story with us, you become part of a movement to break stigma, offer hope, and show others that recovery is possible. Your voice could be precisely what someone else needs to hear, and to let them know that They Are Not Alone.

About Life After

An innovative, survivor-driven docu-series from Vancouver, Canada, offering stories of hope, resilience, and transformation. We illuminate a path for the millions of humans facing mental health challenges today. Our show features raw, personal stories of recovery, allowing viewers to relate and learn about various treatment paths in an unbiased, non-commercialized manner. This program will expand to become a broader platform for mental health education and awareness, with proceeds donated to support mental health initiatives and care for those who cannot afford it.

Who are we looking for?

Seeking individuals of all ages who have experienced different mental health challenges and found their unique path to healing.
